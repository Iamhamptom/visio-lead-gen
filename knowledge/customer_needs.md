# Customer Needs Tank (Knowledge Base)

This document defines the common scenarios users face and the specific data points the AI needs to gather to provide the best assistance.

## 1. üéµ Song/EP Release (The "Drop")
**User Intent:** "I'm dropping a song", "releasing a single", "new EP coming out"
**Goal:** Promote music to blogs, influencers, and curators.

**AI Must Ask:**
1.  **Genre/Vibe:** (e.g., Amapiano, Hip-Hop, Afro House) - *Crucial for selecting the right blogs.*
2.  **Artist Name:** Who is the artist?
3.  **Release Date:** Is it out now or upcoming? (affects urgency).
4.  **Key Assets:** Do you have a Press Release or EPK ready? (If not, AI might suggest creating one).

**Target Data:**
- Music Blogs / Review Sites
- TikTok Influencers (for challenges)
- Radio Compilers (if database has them)
- Playlist Curators

## 2. üìÖ Event Promotion
**User Intent:** "Hosting a party", "club night", "launch event", "concert"
**Goal:** Drive ticket sales and awareness.

**AI Must Ask:**
1.  **Location:** Which city? (JHB, CPT, DBN) - *To find local influencers.*
2.  **Date:** When is it?
3.  **Vibe/Theme:** (e.g., Street culture, High-end, Underground).
4.  **Budget:** Paid promo or barter? (Helps filter for nano vs. mega influencers).

**Target Data:**
- Lifestyle Influencers
- Event Pages / "What's On" Guides
- Local Culture Pages (e.g., "Kasi Vibes")

## 3. ü§ù Brand Partnership / Sponsorship
**User Intent:** "I need influencers for my brand", "looking for ambassadors"
**Goal:** Find creators to represent a product or service.

**AI Must Ask:**
1.  **Brand/Product:** What are you selling? (Fashion, Tech, Alcohol, etc.)
2.  **Target Audience:** Who are you trying to reach? (Youth, Professionals, etc.)
3.  **Campaign Goal:** Brand awareness or Sales/Conversions?

**Target Data:**
- Verified Influencers with high engagement
- Niche content creators

## 4. üì∞ General PR / Visibility
**User Intent:** "I want to get verified", "need more followers", "build my profile"
**Goal:** General reputation management and visibility.

**AI Must Ask:**
1.  **Current Status:** Are you an artist, brand, or public figure?
2.  **Story:** What is your unique angle or story?

**Target Data:**
- Online Magazines
- Interview Platforms (Podcasts, YouTube show hosts)
