# Market Intelligence Skills

## South Africa Music Market
- **Dominant genres**: Amapiano, Afro House, Gqom, SA Hip-Hop, Kwaito
- **Key platforms**: Spotify (growing), Apple Music, TikTok, YouTube
- **Top publications**: Fakaza, SA Hip Hop Mag, Slikour, Okay Africa, Channel O
- **Radio**: Metro FM, Ukhozi FM, 5FM, Good Hope FM
- **Influencer pricing**: Nano (5K-50K): R500-R2000, Micro (50K-200K): R2000-R8000, Macro (200K+): R8000+

## UK Music Market
- **Dominant genres**: Drill, Grime, Afrobeats UK, Pop, R&B, Garage
- **Key platforms**: Spotify, YouTube, Instagram, SoundCloud
- **Top publications**: GRM Daily, Link Up TV, SBTV, Complex UK, NME
- **Radio**: BBC Radio 1/1Xtra, Capital Xtra, Rinse FM, Reprezent
- **Influencer pricing**: Nano: £100-£500, Micro: £500-£2000, Macro: £2000+

## USA Music Market
- **Dominant genres**: Hip-Hop, R&B, Pop, Latin, Country, Indie
- **Key platforms**: Spotify, Apple Music, YouTube, TikTok, SoundCloud
- **Top publications**: Pitchfork, Complex, XXL, HotNewHipHop, The FADER
- **Radio**: iHeartRadio network, SiriusXM, college radio
- **Influencer pricing**: Nano: $100-$500, Micro: $500-$5000, Macro: $5000+
