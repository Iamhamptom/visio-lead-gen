# Campaign Planning Skills

## Release Campaign Timeline (Standard)

### 6 Weeks Before Release
- Finalize artwork, press photos, EPK
- Write press release (AP style)
- Start building media list (curators, blogs, radio)

### 4 Weeks Before Release
- Submit to DSPs (Spotify for Artists, Apple Music Connect)
- Pitch editorial playlists
- Begin PR outreach to long-lead publications

### 3 Weeks Before Release
- Send press releases to all media contacts
- Pitch blogs and online magazines
- Start influencer outreach for launch day content

### 2 Weeks Before Release
- Social media teasers begin
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Pre-save campaign launch

### 1 Week Before Release
- Final push on social media
- Confirm all placements
- Prepare release day content calendar

### Release Day & Week After
- Release day social media blitz
- Share all placements and features
- Thank supporters
- Begin follow-up outreach for late coverage

## Budget Allocation Guide
| Budget Level | PR/Outreach | Influencers | Ads | Content |
|-------------|-------------|-------------|-----|---------|
| Low (<R5K) | 40% | 20% | 20% | 20% |
| Medium (R5K-R20K) | 30% | 30% | 25% | 15% |
| High (R20K+) | 25% | 35% | 25% | 15% |
