# Pitch Writing Skills

## Email Pitch Framework (40%+ Open Rate)

### Subject Line Rules
- Under 50 characters
- Include genre or artist hook
- Create curiosity, not clickbait
- Examples:
  - "New Amapiano x Drill crossover - exclusive"
  - "SA artist featured by [known curator] - similar vibe"
  - "[Artist] - perfect fit for [specific playlist]"

### Email Structure
1. **Personal hook** (1 sentence): Reference their recent work/post
2. **The pitch** (2-3 sentences): What you want, why it fits their audience
3. **Assets**: Private SoundCloud/Dropbox link (never attach MP3s)
4. **CTA**: Clear, specific ask
5. **Total length**: Under 150 words

### DM Pitch Framework
- Even shorter: 2-3 sentences max
- Lead with the value for THEM
- Include a direct link
- Don't be salesy — be genuine

## Follow-up Cadence
- First follow-up: 5-7 days after initial pitch
- Maximum 2 follow-ups total
- Each follow-up must add NEW value (new press coverage, new streaming milestone, etc.)
- If no response after 2 follow-ups, move on
